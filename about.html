What is special education?

Special education refers to a range of services that can be provided to students in different ways and in different settings. The Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) is a federal law that defines and regulates special education. The law requires public schools to provide special education services to children between ages 3 and 21 who meet certain criteria. There are 13 categories covered under IDEA:
